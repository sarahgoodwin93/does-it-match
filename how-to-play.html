Play Game

'Does it Match'

  • Find the 4 images that share something in common
  • Click on the images you want to select and press 'submit'
  • If you are correct you will move up a level
  • If you are wrong, you will get 2 more chances to win!

Each level has only 1 correct match, good luck!
